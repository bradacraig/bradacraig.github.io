About Me

Born and raised in Auckland, NZ, music has been a big part of my life for the past 10 years. I’ve been lucky enough to play guitar and sing in a handful of bands that have played shows around the world.

While I’m still quietly chasing the music dream, my current focus is studying to become a software developer at Dev Academy.

I’m looking forward to learning new skills and meeting new people along this journey. My ultimate goal is to find a job where I can put my new skills to use while still having time for music.
